How do I have time for so many projects?

In my decision for a career path and major, I have selected a degree that allows me to learn with hands on experience with invention and problem solving, particularly in the team setting. The majority of my classes involve large scale and heavily weighted group projects to design various electrical, mechanical, and robotic systems. These classes give me practical work combining the disciplines and knowledge of all three of the above engineering focuses. In addition, the core of my classes at the Polytechnic campus are project-based courses through a flipped classroom, meaning all class time is spent working with a team of engineering students to develop solutions for opportunities we identify in this world. These classes are the crux of my education, giving me physical practice with the robotics field and the numerous disciplines that comprise it.
