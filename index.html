Microbike

A project that I did my sophomore year of college where I designed and manufactured a small electric motorcycle that could reach speeds of 35mph.

Self-Watering Planter

A project that I completed in the fall semester of my junior year that measured soil moisture levels and ambient room lighting in order to keep a plant healthy. It would activate a pump to water the plant if the soil moisture levels were too low and would adjust the light coming from the on-board LEDs to make up for the lack of light in the room (if necessary).
